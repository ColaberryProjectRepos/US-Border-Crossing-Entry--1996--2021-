Create group on Methods of crossing related to each other. 9


1- Expand the table in the Fields tab

2- Select the Method column on the elipsis right click to new group.

 3-Define Name, as Crossing Methods.

 4-CTRL and Select the Columns you wanted to Group

5- Name the group in the highlighted part

6- Click on Group and Enter

7- Click OK.

8- You will see another column which grouped under the table in the fields tab.
